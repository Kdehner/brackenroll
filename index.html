Before You Begin

— ✦ —

This campaign is built around moral complexity and genuine choice. There are no pure heroes or pure villains here — just people doing desperate things in desperate circumstances.

You will be asked to choose sides. You will find reasons to doubt your choice. The world will not reward you for playing it safe.

The forest is listening. And it remembers.
